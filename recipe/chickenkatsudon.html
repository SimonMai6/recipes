Chicken Katusdon

Description

This recipe of the chicken katsudon is very delicious. It has everything you need in one bowl, a crispy chicken katsu, an egg covering the rice, sweet and savory sauce that coats the rice below, and fluffy rice.

Ingredients

For the Chicken Katsu

  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 large egg (beaten)
  • 1 cup panko
  • 7 oz chicken tenders
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • 3 cups neutral oil

For Seasonings

  • ⅔ cup dashi
  • 1 Tbsp sake
  • 1 Tbsp mirin
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar

Steps

For the Chicken Katsu

  1. Cover the chicken tenders with the beaten egg and cover it with panko.
  2. Pour oil into pot or pan and heat until 340ºF.
  3. Add the coated chicken tenders into the oil and wait until ready, then take out and let it sit out to cool.

For the Chicken Katsudon

  1. Add the dashi, sake, soy sauce, mirin, sugar, and onion into a saucepan.
  2. Once the onions are wilted, lay the Chicken Katsu on top and then add the beaten eggs on top of the Chicken Katsu and cover the pan.
  3. When the egg is finished, transfer the finished product onto a bowl full of rice and enjoy.
