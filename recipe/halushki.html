Halushki

Description

Polish dish combining bacon, fried cabbage and egg noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bacon
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 (16 ounce) package egg noodles
  • 1 head cabbage, sliced
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Add bacon and onion in a skillet and cook until done.
  2. Boil water in large pot with salt and cook egg noodles until firm to the bite and drain.
  3. Transfer the bacon and onions into the pot and mix together. Enjoy!
